About Valerie

Tough beginnings. I started life as a latch-key kid living in a south-side Chicago ghetto. But my Momma taught me to be TUFF (Tenacious, Uncommon, Fireproof, Fit) and transformed me into the TUFF Mama people know me as today.

Unlikely pathways. After earning my STEM degree from the University of Michigan, I accepted the challenge to first servce as an Air Force officer and then came to the real challenge: Mother of 9. I applied my core team values I learned on active duty to manage my children - tons of love added, of course. Equipping women to love themselves and to competently guide their children - that's what I'm about.

Incredible impact. I retained my indomitable soldier spirit and now also take non-profits and corporate teams to their next best levels as a resiliency educator, business communicator, course developer, and wickedly-creative strategist. Enriching professionals and teams to deliver their best - that's my sweet spot.

I deliver a special brand of "TUFF" for people who won't let challenges keep them down.
